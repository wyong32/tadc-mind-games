# TADC Mind Games

**Official player hub:** [https://tadcmindgames.org](https://tadcmindgames.org)

TADC Mind Games is a fan-made horror visual novel inspired by *The Amazing Digital Circus*. The game puts you inside a strange digital circus where every answer can push the story toward control, panic, defiance, or collapse. This site is an independent player hub where you can play online, track routes, compare endings, and find related horror games in your browser.

> **Disclaimer:** This is an independent fan site. It is not affiliated with PITCHBOX Studios, Glitch Productions, or Gooseworx.

---

## About the Game

TADC Mind Games is a choice-driven horror visual novel built around pressure, mood, and replay. Instead of chasing every branch at once, the game rewards players who read the room, notice character signals, and replay with a clear plan.

The current build centers on three visible characters:

- **Caine** — control, praise, and obedience pressure
- **Pomni** — panic, escape instinct, and emotional tension
- **Kinger** — odd clues, slower detours, and unsettling room details

You can play in the browser on this site, or through downloadable builds on Windows and macOS.

## Key Features

- **Browser H5 play** — launch the game directly from the homepage without leaving the site
- **Five known ending paths** — trust Caine, defy Caine, support Pomni, follow Kinger, or mix choices for uncertain routes
- **Choice-based horror** — dialogue decisions shape mood, danger, and ending direction
- **Route planning tools** — walkthrough, endings tracker, choice matrix, and character notes in one place
- **Replay-friendly design** — short runs and clear replay logic make ending hunting easier
- **Related horror picks** — browser games for players who want more tension after a TADC route

## Quick Start Guide

1. **Play one blind run first**  
   Open [Play Online](https://tadcmindgames.org/#play) and finish one route before trying to unlock every ending.

2. **Pick a replay mood**  
   Decide whether your next run will focus on trust, doubt, panic, curiosity, or delay.

3. **Change one sharp choice**  
   Return to the answer that felt most uncomfortable and try the opposite tone.

4. **Track the ending pull**  
   Once the scene shifts, stay with that mood until the route becomes clear.

5. **Use the guide pages**  
   Move from the [Walkthrough](https://tadcmindgames.org/walkthrough/) to [Endings](https://tadcmindgames.org/endings/) and [Choices](https://tadcmindgames.org/choices/) as you start hunting specific outcomes.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I play TADC Mind Games online?

Yes. Go to [https://tadcmindgames.org/#play](https://tadcmindgames.org/#play) and press **Play Game** to load the browser version on the site.

### How many endings are in the current build?

The current build has **five known ending paths**. Use the [Endings page](https://tadcmindgames.org/endings/) to compare them and plan replays.

### Which characters matter most for routes?

The visible cast currently centers on **Caine**, **Pomni**, and **Kinger**. The [Characters page](https://tadcmindgames.org/characters/) explains how each one shapes route pressure and ending direction.

### Where should I go after my first ending?

If you want another outcome, open the [Endings guide](https://tadcmindgames.org/endings/). If you remember the choice that caused trouble, use the [Choices page](https://tadcmindgames.org/choices/) to compare risk types before replaying.

### What is the best first route?

A cautious Caine-focused run is a strong starting point. It introduces the game's pressure without forcing you to chase every hidden branch immediately. See the [Walkthrough](https://tadcmindgames.org/walkthrough/) for a full replay plan.

### Is this an official TADC site?

No. This is an independent H5 game and guide site for players. TADC Mind Games is a fan-made horror visual novel inspired by *The Amazing Digital Circus*.

### Are the related games official too?

No. Related game pages recommend third-party browser titles for players who enjoy horror with pressure, replay value, or escape tension. Each related game page includes its own notes and play context.

### How do I report outdated route info?

Use the [Contact Us page](https://tadcmindgames.org/legal/contact-us/) to send corrections, outdated build notes, or content suggestions.
